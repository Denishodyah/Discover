grand canyon

The Grand Canyon is a giant canyon in the northern part of the state of Arizona, USA. It is formed by the Colorado River and is about 446 km long and up to 1.8 km deep. The Grand Canyon is one of the world's most impressive natural wonders and a popular tourist destination, attracting millions of visitors each year...

Mount Rushmore

Mount Rushmore is a famous mountain in the United States, located in the state of South Dakota. On the slope of this mountain there are huge sculptures of four US presidents: George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Abraham Lincoln and Theodore Roosevelt.

learn more

Death Valley

Death Valley is the largest national park in the United States, located in California and Nevada. This unique place is known for its extreme climate, high temperatures and low humidity, making it one of the hottest and driest regions on Earth.

learn more

Devil's Tower

Devil's Tower is a unique natural formation in the state of Wyoming, USA. It is a monolithic formation about 386 meters high, consisting of vertical columns of basalt. Devil's Tower is the first national monument in the United States and one of the most famous geologic sites in the world.

learn more

Park Glacier

Glacier National Park is a spectacular national park located in the state of Montana, USA. It stretches over 4,000 square kilometers and offers amazing natural beauty including mountain peaks, lakes, glaciers, forests and wildlife.
